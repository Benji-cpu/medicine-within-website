The Medicine

What is Kambo?

Kambo is the secretion of the giant monkey frog (Phyllomedusa bicolor), native to the Amazon rainforest. For thousands of years, indigenous tribes have used this powerful medicine for healing, strength, and clarity.

Applied through small points on the skin, Kambo rapidly purges toxins, clears energetic blockages, and resets the nervous system. The experience is intense but transformative—many describe feeling "reborn" after ceremony.

  • Detoxifies the liver, intestines, and lymphatic system
  • Clears mental fog and emotional stagnation
  • Boosts immune function and energy levels
  • Releases stored trauma from the body
  • Sharpens intuition and mental clarity

    Common Questions

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Everything you need to know about Kambo ceremonies, preparation, and what to expect.

    Who should not receive Kambo? (Contraindications)

    Kambo is powerful medicine that is not suitable for everyone. Kambo is NOT suitable if you:

    • Have serious heart conditions or have had a heart attack
    • Are taking blood pressure medication or blood thinners
    • Have had a stroke or brain hemorrhage
    • Have epilepsy or serious mental health conditions
    • Are pregnant, breastfeeding, or trying to conceive
    • Have Addison's disease or severe kidney/liver problems
    • Are under 18 years of age
    • Have undergone chemotherapy in the last 6 weeks
    • Are taking immunosuppressants
    • Have had major surgery in the last 2 months

    Medical Screening Required: All participants must complete a comprehensive medical intake form before being accepted into ceremony. This is non-negotiable. Your safety is my highest priority.

    How long does the ceremony last?

    Group ceremonies typically last 3-4 hours, including preparation, the ceremony itself, and integration time. Private ceremonies can be tailored to your needs and may take longer.

    What should I bring to the ceremony?

    Wear comfortable, loose-fitting clothing. Bring a water bottle, a change of clothes (in case you sweat during the purge), a light snack for after, and any items that help you feel grounded (crystals, journal, etc.).

    How many points are typically applied?

    The number of points varies based on your experience, body size, and intention. First-time participants usually start with 3-5 points. During your discovery call, we'll discuss what's appropriate for you.

    Is Kambo safe?

    When administered by a trained practitioner following proper protocols, Kambo is generally safe. However, it has strict contraindications. All participants must complete a comprehensive medical screening before being accepted. Safety is my highest priority.

    What does the purge feel like?

    The purge typically begins within minutes. You may experience heat, swelling in the face, nausea, and vomiting. This is normal and part of the cleansing process. The intense phase usually lasts 20-40 minutes, after which you'll feel a sense of relief and clarity.

    How often can I receive Kambo?

    It's recommended to wait at least 3-4 weeks between ceremonies to allow your body to fully integrate the medicine. Some people do a series of 3 ceremonies over several months for deeper healing, while others do it seasonally for maintenance.
