Frequently Asked Questions

Is this therapy or coaching?

This is neither traditional therapy nor conventional coaching. It's a unique blend of somatic practices, medicine work, embodiment, and deep transformation. The focus is on embodiment and reconnection rather than analysis. If you're dealing with severe trauma or mental health issues, please seek appropriate professional support first.

Do I need experience with Kambo or Tantra?

No prior experience is necessary. I'll guide you through everything step by step. You do need to be open to medicine work and willing to explore practices that may be outside your comfort zone. If you have medical contraindications for Kambo, we can discuss alternatives.

How is this different from other programs I've tried?

This mentorship is body-based, not just intellectual. We don't just talk about change‚Äîwe feel it, we move it, we embody it. With only 5 women at a time, you get personalized attention that's impossible in larger programs.
